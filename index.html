Dining on Campus at UNCG

Your best bet to finding the best places to eat near the Petty Building!

As a 4th year UNCG Student, I know how challenging it is to balance time between classes and finding good places to eat. This blog will tell you my top 3 restaurants near the petty building, and my favorite foods from those places. These are very convinient to go to in case you dont have a meal plan or arent craving foods from the Dining Hall. I hope you find this Blog useful for you during your time at UNCG.

Restaurant 1: Chipotle Mexican Grill

This restaurant is located about 1/2 a mile away from petty building, and is known for its burrito bowls. It is open Daily from 10:45AM to 11:00PM, satifying your late dinner cravings.

1. Chipotle Burrito

Made to Order Burrito.

2. Chipotle Burrito Bowl

Made to Order Burrito Bowl. Can add Tortilla for +0.50$

3. Chipotle Quesadilla

Made to Order Quesadilla.

Restaurant 2: Bojangle's

This restaurant is located about 1/2 a mile away from the Petty Building as well, and is know for its chicken and biscuits. This location is open Sundays from 9:30AM-8:00PM and Monday-Friday from 7:30AM-10:00PM.

1. 4-Piece Supreme Combo

Includes a biscuit, side of your choice, and a drink.

2. Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Biscuit Combo

The best biscuit combo in their menu.

Bo's Chicken Sandwich Combo

includes a side of your choice and a drink.

Restaurant 3: Panda Express

This restaurant is located in the Moran Commons building, which is across from the petty building, making it the closest restaurant nearby. It is open Sundays-Mondays, whith a variety of hours.

1. 1 side + 2 entree "Plate"

Plate option larger than the bowl.

2. Double Protein Plate

Plate option with plenty of protein.

Chicken Egg Rolls

Egg Rolls by the order of 1. The best in the game.

Fun Fact

Some restaurants, like the Bojangle's and Panda Express acually accept flex, which should be included in any default meal plan, which means that you dont have to spend your own money all the time! Restaurants, like Chipotle offer student discounts, like the Chipotle-U program.
